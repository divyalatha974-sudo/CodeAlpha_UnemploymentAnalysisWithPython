💡 Key Insights & Policy Recommendations
📈
Covid-19 Unemployment Spike
National unemployment jumped from 9.76% to 13.28% post-lockdown — a 36.1% relative increase. May 2020 was the peak at 23.24% national average with Puducherry hitting a record 75.85%.
🧭
Regional Disparity
North India averaged 15.89% unemployment — nearly double that of West India at 8.24%. This reflects structural differences in industry composition, with North being more dependent on informal labour markets.
🔄
Recovery Pattern
June–August 2020 showed strong recovery as unlock phases began. However, rates never returned to pre-COVID levels by November 2020, suggesting lasting structural damage to employment, especially in tourism and hospitality sectors.
📌
Policy Recommendations
North & East India need targeted MGNREGA expansion. Urban areas need gig economy regulation and social protection floors. States like Puducherry and Tamil Nadu need tourism sector revival packages to rebuild employment.
